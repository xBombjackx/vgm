## 1. Synthesis of Feedback
I have analyzed reviews from **10 Reader Personas** (ranging from "Lofi Producers" to "Casual Gamers") and **3 Publisher Personas** (Editor, Marketer, Academic).

### **The Consensus**
*   **The Content is Strong:** The core "Hybrid" thesis (Theory + Engineering) is validated. The technical chapters (Genesis, SNES, Tracker) are the high points.
*   **The Presentation Needs Work:** The text is good, but the *support* is lacking. Readers are demanding visual aids and downloadable files.
*   **The Tone is mostly correct:** "Accessible Academic" works for 80% of the audience, though it alienates casuals (which is intentional).
